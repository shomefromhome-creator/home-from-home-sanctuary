How It Began

Born from love.
Built on purpose.

Home From Home Sanctuary didn't begin as a business plan. It began as a lifeline — at the most painful point of my life, when I needed something to hold on to.

After losing my daughter, it was a rescue animal who gave me a reason to keep going. That bond — between a broken heart and an animal who needed me as much as I needed them — became the foundation of everything I now do.

For over thirty-five years I have cared for animals of every species, earning qualifications, awards and — more importantly — the trust of hundreds of families across Hertfordshire and beyond. But the dream has always been bigger than pet care.

The dream is a sanctuary. A permanent, healing place where rescued animals are safe, nurtured, and loved. Where the bond that saved me can save others too.

The bond between a person in pain and an animal in need is one of the most powerful forces I have ever witnessed. I want to create a place where that healing can happen for every animal who needs it.

— Jo, Founder, Home From Home Sanctuary
The Vision

What we are building

🐕

Rescue & Rehabilitation

A safe intake facility for dogs, cats, small animals, poultry, exotics and every species in between — with proper veterinary care, enrichment and time to heal.

🌿

Healing Retreat

A calm, green space where animals and people can recover together — integrating the proven healing power of the human-animal bond into structured wellbeing programmes.

🏡

Permanent Land

We are actively seeking land across Hertfordshire and the surrounding counties — a permanent home that no lease can threaten and no landlord can end.

Land Wanted

Do you have land
we could call home?

We are searching for a plot of land — ideally in Hertfordshire or the surrounding counties — that could become the permanent home of the sanctuary. We are open to all conversations: purchase, long lease, donation or partnership.

We are looking for:

  • Minimum 2–5 acres (larger always considered)
  • Hertfordshire, Bedfordshire, Buckinghamshire or North London
  • Outbuildings, stabling or agricultural land welcome
  • Any condition considered
  • Private sales, gifts or long leases all welcomed

Know of somewhere?

If you own land, know someone who does, or have any connection that could help — we would love to hear from you. Every conversation is treated in complete confidence.
