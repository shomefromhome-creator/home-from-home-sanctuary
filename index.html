Our Approach

I'm Jo.

For over thirty-five years, animals have been the constant thread through my life — first as a passion, now as a profession I take as seriously as any I could imagine.

Every client I work with has one point of contact: me. Not a rotating pool of sitters, not an app matching you to whoever's free that day. I meet you and your pets in your home before any booking is confirmed, and I build a care plan around your routine — not the other way round.

I care for dogs, cats, small mammals, poultry and exotics — including rescued reptiles — and I extend that same care to your home: post, plants, deliveries, and the small details that matter while you're away.

— Jo, Home From Home Sanctuary
